Background

As a CTO/Head of IT (for a Tesco company) I have been through the lifecycle of an online subscription business – from start-up through acquisition, expansion and on to multi-million revenue enterprise-level operation.

To do this, I have built networks and installed servers, written application code, set up and managed databases, recruited and line-managed over 40 tech staff and been closely bound to the business needs along the way, including negotiation on licencing and SLAs.

In addition to 30 years of IT experience, I have experience of business development, lead sourcing and the marketing and face-to-face selling of physical products and software development services, as well as a number of directorships along the way.
