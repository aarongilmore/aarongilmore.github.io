About

I've been making things on the Web since the mid-2000s; firstly playing around with Photoshop and Dreamweaver and quickly graduating to studying web design as a discipline.

I also build website from time-to-time, at the very least I keep up-to-date with current web standards and frameworks so I understand the work my fellow web developers do.

When I'm not making websites I'm usually reading books on phycology, travel writing, urban planning, and computer history. Sometimes lifting heavy weights, often on a plane or train. Want to chat over a coffee sometime?
