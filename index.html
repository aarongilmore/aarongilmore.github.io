Aaron Gilmore, Designer and Developer

I'm an independent designer and developer based in Ireland, and a student of Interactive Design at the University of Ulster. I previously worked as a Product Design Intern for Brewbot.

I'm primarily a designer — but I believe that role comes with the responsibilty of understaing how to build the products I design.

I'm available for freelance work. If you want to discuss a project, you can email me at aaron@aarongilmore.com or tweet me @aarondgilmore.

Brewbot · Product Design Intern

2013 - 2014

www.brewbot.io

Brewbot uses sensor technology to track and automate the brewing process. Simply follow the instructions in the app, add the ingredients when prompted and, hey presto, you can call yourself a brewer.

In 2014, Brewbot was accepted into the Techstars program in Austin, TX. It has been featured on Techcrunch, Mashable, Fast Company, and Offscreen.
