Brewbot

Brewbot is a self-contained brewing machine that is controlled and monitored using a smartphone app.

Brewbot was brought to life thanks to the 381 people who backed the project on Kickstarter. During the 30-day campaign Brewbot was featured in Fast Company, New Scientist, NBC News, the Discovery Channel and more.
