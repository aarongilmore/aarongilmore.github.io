Slim —

2014–2015 Design, development

A simple weight and BMI tracking app for iOS. My final year university dissertation.

The App

In my final year of university I really wanted to learn something new, so I set myself the task of designing and building a native iOS application.

Born out of frustration with the complicated and poorly designed weight-tracking apps on the iPhone, Slim's aim was to provide a well designed solution to weight tracking on iOS.
